###在Fedora 21中安装

  1. RPM: freetype-infinality-2.4.12-1.20130514_01.fc21.x86_64.rpm
  2. RPM: fontconfig-infinality-1-20120615_1.noarch.rpm

再安装gnome-tweak-tool来设置slight和rgb，安装包

RPM: gnome-tweak-tool
